Draws a tile-shaped marker that follows your mouse over the game. Calibrate: press the button, then click the ground in game — the addon measures the game's yellow destination marker and adopts its exact size and proportions. Recalibrate after zooming or tilting the camera. Note: Alt1 cannot read the camera, so the marker follows your cursor smoothly — it cannot snap tile-to-tile like the game's own marker.
